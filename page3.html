Superlife Farm

About Us

Superlife Farm exists to identify and promote the many ways in which sustainable agricultural development can be advanced In Gambia. With one shared voice, Superlife Farm highlights the importance of improving farmers’ livelihoods as well as the important contribution that agriculture can make to related global issues such as food security, climate change, and biodiversity. It also aims to build synergies amongst its supporters in promoting Superlife Farm's mission.

Returning farmers to the centre of policy decisions is fundamental to sustainable development. Governments, businesses, scientists and civil society groups must focus attention on the source of our food security. All these groups must work together to enable the many millions of farm families, especially smallholders, to grow more crops sustainably through effective markets, more collaborative research and committed knowledge sharing.

Superlife Farm's Mission

Superlife Farm supporters all endorse a broad-based framework consisting of six interlinked principles for sustainable development:
1. Safeguarding natural resources
2. Sharing knowledge
3. Building local access and capacity
4. Protecting harvests
5. Enabling access to markets
6. Prioritising research imperatives
